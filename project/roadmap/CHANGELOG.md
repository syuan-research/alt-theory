# Alt Theory Changelog

Current pilot/product-visible changelog. The public page mirrors these short
entries at `alt-theory-app/web-server/public/changelog.html`.

## v0.5.5 - 2026-06-23

Wider inspector panel, Connecting/Thinking chat status, and more reliable reconnect after brief disconnects.

Details:

- Right panel resize range increased so inspector notes and records stay readable at wider widths.
- Chat stream shows server-driven `Connecting…` and `Thinking…` rows during model turns (distinct from tool-progress labels).
- WebSocket reconnect restores the active session without a full page refresh.

## v0.5.4 - 2026-06-22

## v0.5.3 - 2026-06-20

## v0.5.1 - 2026-06-18
