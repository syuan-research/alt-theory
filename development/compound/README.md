# Development Compound

Project-wide CodeStable-derived archive records live here:

- decisions
- learnings
- tricks
- explores

This is a transitional collection retained because its records still explain
implemented engineering choices. It is not a home for new plans, handoffs, or
process logs. Historical records may mention private source material that is
intentionally absent from the public repository.

Reusable upstream research on coding-agent conversation stores and readers is
indexed in `research-agent-session-history/`. Product behavior belongs in
`development/architecture/`, not in those research factsheets.
