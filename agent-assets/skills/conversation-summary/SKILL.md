# Conversation Summary

Summarize the conversation using these principles:

1. Mark uncertainty explicitly and distinguish claims based on internal model knowledge from claims established in the conversation.
2. Do not add substantial recommendations or new directions unless the user requested them or they were actually discussed.
3. Include enough background, decisions, reasons, and unresolved points for the summary to stand alone.
